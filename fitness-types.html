Popular Fitness Types

Cardiovascular Exercise

Cardio exercises increase heart rate and improve cardiovascular health. Examples include running, cycling, and swimming.

Strength Training

Strength training involves resistance exercises to build muscle strength and tone. Examples include weightlifting and bodyweight exercises.

Flexibility Training

Flexibility exercises enhance range of motion and reduce the risk of injuries. Examples include yoga and stretching routines.

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

HIIT combines short bursts of intense exercise with periods of rest or lower intensity. Examples include sprint intervals and circuit training.
